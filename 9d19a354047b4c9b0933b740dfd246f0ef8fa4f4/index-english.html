Parish Nuestra Señora del Pilar

The greatest altar of the Nuestra Señora del Pilar church is decorated with a beautiful colonial altarpiece from the XVIII century, donated by the De la Borda family, who brought it from the San Francisco Javier church, located in the San José de Nazca estate. It’s an extraordinary work of baroque style made of wood, covered in gold of 15 meters high and 9.50 meters wide.

Casa Paz Soldan

The Paz Soldán house is located in Jr. De la Unión 1039 Cercado de Lima. There are two main areas known as "casa grande" and "casa chica", connected by interior passages. The architecture is most well known for the presence of a "zaguán" with an arch over two stone pillars, corridors, wide spaces and many rooms.
