Next Stop, Urubamba!

The Perfect Place!

When we arrived we were looking for the perfect spot to take pictures. I did not understand why Marco was so worried in finding a good spot!

The Proposal...

We finally found a great spot; Marco took out a small album that he had prepared for the occasion. When I opened it, I started reading what it said and I looked at the pictures that were in it. From the night, we met each other all the way to the present day. I could not hold the tears back when I arrived to the last page, I looked at him and he was on one knee asking me to marry him.

She Said Yes!

Logically I accepted! After drying our tears of happiness and hugging each other, I finally understood why we needed to find a great spot for the pictures!

Happiness!
